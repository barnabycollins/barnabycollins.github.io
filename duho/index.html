Durham University Hill Orchestra

Durham University Hill Orchestra is one of the main orchestras at Durham University. It is unique in terms of its total inclusivity – the orchestra is completely non-auditioned and welcomes orchestral players of any level and experience. DUHO enjoys a relaxed and friendly approach to tackling exciting concert repertoire, ranging from classic film scores such as The Empire Strikes Back, E.T. and The Lord of the Rings to orchestral greats, including Schubert’s ‘Unfinished’ Symphony and Sibelius’ Finlandia.

With a history of passionate and inspiring conductors, great concert opportunities in beautiful venues, yearly tours and fun and imaginative socials, DUHO offers the perfect way to keep the pleasure of orchestral music-making alive at Durham University.

Join Us

Membership of The Hill Orchestra is open to any member of Durham University and costs just £12 per year. Being a member has many perks, such as discounted Hill Orchestra merchandise, biscuits at rehearsals, and recognition of your hard work through inclusion in the concert programmes.

Alternatively, if you have any questions, feel free to contact us at the society email address.
